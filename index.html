About Me

I am assistant professor (lecturer) of political science at King's College London in the Department of Political Economy. I received my PhD in 2018 from the European University Institute, where I was awarded Josip Peić Prize for the best thesis in Political and Social Sciences. My research focuses on comparative politics and political economy. I am specially interested in political and intergroup conflict. Please find my CV here.
